About Me

A growing engineer who has the technical knowledge of how things work in the world of zero's and one's, ready with an edge to dive into the design process to discover, ideate and build some cool product.

Who is this man, dude, myth or mega developer?

Well that's enough of talking about myself in the third person. I am an award winning open source contributor and web developer, always striving for learning and building up new things, because the noblest pleasure is the joy of self-satisfaction and understanding.

I am a learning addict, visionary and risk taker. Innovation is my passion. Being logically minded and creative at heart, I create beautiful products. I love spending time on fixing little details and optimizing products. I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, beautiful and intuitive interface designs.

I like working in teams as you'll learn faster and much more and the final product comes out to be the best. As the saying goes: "None of us is as smart as all of us.".

Based in New Delhi, India, and a student at Northern India Engineering College, I am up for any freelance project that tend to make me learn something new.
